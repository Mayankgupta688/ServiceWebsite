About Us

It didn’t take long for the team to come up with a name that shall set new benchmarks for tech support websites in the entire world — Orchetech.The first thing that Orchetech focussed on, was quality and convenience for the end users. It wasn’t easy, after all, to outmatch the competition just from the word go. We, therefore, set goals, and gradually started getting the desired results. But to keep the costs down, we had to include one important facet in our company — affordability. This was achieved by streamlining the various channels of work and thereby achieving a common link. With this in mind, we created a firm that was supposed to be friendly and be as convenient for as for home-users.
